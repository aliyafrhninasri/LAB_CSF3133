About Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT)

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a public university located in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu, Malaysia. It was initially established as the Centre for Fisheries and Marine Science of Universiti Putra Malaysia and later evolved into an independent university with strong emphasis on marine science and environmental studies.

UMT offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in marine-related fields, environmental science, food technology, engineering and social sciences. The university is actively involved in research, community engagement, and international collaborations.
